2020 Economic Summit

Cache Valley Chamber of Commerce, in conjunction with valuable supporting partners, is proud to present the first annual Greater Cache Valley Economic Summit! The GCV Economic Summit is replacing the Cache Business Summit successfully held annually for several years, attracting the area's top business leaders. This expansion to the GCV Economic Summit will expand to not only include top business leaders but also top community and economic leaders from Norther Utah and Southern Idaho. With Fantastic keynotes from local and state leaders to more hands on breakouts sessions you are sure to gain valuable insights. Breakfast and lunch will be provided, and you won't want to miss the popular "Taste of Greater Cache Valley" where several local restaurants come together to offer samples of their favorite dishes! We look forward to seeing you there!
